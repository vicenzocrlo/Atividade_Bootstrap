Who We are

Raccoon Guardians is a non-profit organization dedicated to the protection, rehabilitation, and conservation of raccoons and their natural habitats.
Founded by wildlife enthusiasts, our mission is to ensure these intelligent and charismatic animals can thrive in harmony with human communities.

Our Mission


We believe in the power of peaceful coexistence. Our work is driven by three main pillars:

Rescue & Rehabilitation: Providing medical care and a safe haven for injured or orphaned raccoons until they are ready to return to the wild.

Education: Informing the public about the ecological importance of raccoons and how to live safely alongside them.

Advocacy: Promoting ethical animal welfare policies and protecting the green spaces they call home.
